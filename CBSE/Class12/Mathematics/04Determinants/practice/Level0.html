6.

Section 2: Properties of Determinants

13.

14.

18.

20.
Evaluate $\begin{vmatrix} 1 & 2 & 3 \\ 10 & 20 & 30 \\ 5 & 7 & 9 \end{vmatrix}$ using properties.
Section 3: Area of a Triangle & Minors/Cofactors
21.
Write the determinant formula for the area of a triangle with vertices $(x_1, y_1)$, $(x_2, y_2)$, and $(x_3, y_3)$.
22.
If three points are collinear, what is the area of the triangle formed by them?
23.
Find the area of the triangle with vertices $(1, 0)$, $(6, 0)$, and $(4, 3)$.
24.

25.

26.

27.

28.

29.
If elements of a row are multiplied by the cofactors of any *other* row, what is the sum of their products?
30.
If elements of a row are multiplied by their *own* corresponding cofactors, what is the sum equal to?
