💡 Pro Hints for Selected Questions:
Q17: Use $\text{Average Mass} = \frac{(35 \times 3) + (37 \times 1)}{3+1}$.
Q28: One molecule of $\text{O}_3$ has $3$ atoms. Total atoms = $0.1 \times N_A \times 3$.
Q32: Moles = $\frac{\text{Total Mass}}{\text{Mass of 1 mole of e}^-}$. Mass of 1 mole $e^-$ = $N_A \times 9.1 \times 10^{-31}$.
Q38: Divide mass % by atomic mass to get mole ratio. $A = 50/10 = 5$, $B = 50/20 = 2.5$. Simple ratio $A:B = 2:1$.
Q42: Moles/Coefficient ratio: $\text{N}_2 = 1/1 = 1$; $\text{H}_2 = 2/3 = 0.66$. $\text{H}_2$ is smaller, hence LR.
