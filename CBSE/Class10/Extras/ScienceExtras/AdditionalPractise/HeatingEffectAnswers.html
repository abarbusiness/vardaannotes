Class: 10 (CBSE) Subject: Science Type: Answer Key & Solutions
SECTION A: HEATING EFFECT & POWER
Q1. Heat Generated
Given: Charge $Q = 96000$ C, Time $t = 1$ hour = 3600 s, Voltage $V = 50$ V.
Heat generated $H = VQ$.
$H = 50 \times 96000 = 4800000$ J = $4.8 \times 10^6$ J.
Alternatively, we can find Current $I = Q/t = 96000/3600 = 80/3$ A.
$H = VIt = 50 \times (80/3) \times 3600 = 50 \times 80 \times 1200 = 4800000$ J.
Answer: $4.8 \times 10^6$ J
