Format (Strictly Follow This)

Sender's Address
(City, State)

Para 1 (Intro): Through the columns of your esteemed newspaper / I am writing to inquire about / I wish to place an order...

Para 2 (Body): Explain the problem/details. Use bullet points if needed.

Para 3 (Conclusion): I hope you will look into this matter / Kindly send the details at the earliest.

Yours faithfully/sincerely,
[Name]
[Designation]

2. Analytical Paragraph (5 Marks)

üìä Key Points

  • Analyze, Don't Copy: Interpret the trends (increase, decrease). Don't just list numbers.
  • Structure: 3 Paragraphs (Intro, Body, Conclusion).

‚ö° Power Vocabulary (For High Marks)

  • Comparison: Whereas, In contrast, Similarly, On the other hand.
  • Trends (Up): Surged, Rocketed, Inclined, Peaked at.
  • Trends (Down): Plummeted, Declined, Dipped, Bottomed out.
  • Stable: Remained constant, Stagnated, Plateaued.

Sample Structure

Introduction: The given [chart/graph/table] illustrates the data regarding [Subject] for the period [Year] to [Year].

Body: It is clearly evident that [Category A] started at [Value] and saw a steady rise, peaking at [Value]. In contrast, [Category B] showed a declining trend. Interestingly, both categories were equal in [Year].

Conclusion: To summarize / In conclusion, majority of people preferred [Category A], while [Category B] was the least popular.

üèÜ Examiner's Marking Scheme (5 Marks)

  • Format (1 Mark): Layout, Sender's Address, Date, Subject, Salutation.
  • Content (2 Marks): Addressing the prompt, key points.
  • Expression (2 Marks): Grammatical accuracy, spelling, coherence.
  • Tip: Stick to 100-120 words. Writing too much increases spelling error risks!
