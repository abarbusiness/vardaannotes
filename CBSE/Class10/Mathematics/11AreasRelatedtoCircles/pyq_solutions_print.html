Q37 (Case Study). Semicircular farmhouse, diameter 70 m, divided by point P with \(\angle PAB = 30°\). Answer: (i) \(\angle POA\)? (ii) Fence for entire land. (iii)(a) Area of Mango region. OR (iii)(b) Fence for region III.
(i) \(\angle POA = 120°\). (ii) Perimeter: \(\pi r + 2r = 180\) m.
(iii)(a) Area I ≈ 753 m².
(iii)(b) Arc + Chord ≈ 71.67 m.
