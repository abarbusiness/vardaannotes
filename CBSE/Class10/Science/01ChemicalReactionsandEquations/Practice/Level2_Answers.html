Module 1: Concept Mix (Chemical Puzzles)
1. X is Copper (Cu). The black compound is Copper Oxide (CuO).
$2Cu(s) + O_2(g) \xrightarrow{\Delta} 2CuO(s)$
2. X is Calcium Oxide (CaO) or Quick Lime. Used in cement.
$CaO(s) + H_2O(l) \rightarrow Ca(OH)_2(aq) + Heat$
Ca(OH)$_2$ is Calcium Hydroxide (basic).
3. X is Zinc (Zn). Y is Hydrogen gas (H$_2$).
$Zn(s) + H_2SO_4(aq) \rightarrow ZnSO_4(aq) + H_2(g)$
4. X is Silver (Ag). The reaction is Photolytic Decomposition.
$2AgCl(s) \xrightarrow{\text{Sunlight}} 2Ag(s) + Cl_2(g)$
5. A is Sodium Bicarbonate (Baking Soda, $NaHCO_3$). On heating it gives $Na_2CO_3$, $H_2O$ and $CO_2$.
6. Salt is Ferrous Sulphate ($FeSO_4 \cdot 7H_2O$). Green crystals.
$2FeSO_4(s) \xrightarrow{\Delta} Fe_2O_3(s) + SO_2(g) + SO_3(g)$
Reddish brown residue is Ferric Oxide ($Fe_2O_3$).
7. (a) 'X' is Calcium Oxide ($CaO$, Quick lime). (b)
$CaO + H_2O \rightarrow Ca(OH)_2$
