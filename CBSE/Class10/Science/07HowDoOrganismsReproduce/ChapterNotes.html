1. DNA & Asexual Modes

Variation Importance: Copying errors create variants. If environment changes drastically (e.g. Temperature rise), variants might survive while original species wipes out. Variation is fuel for Evolution.

Mode Example Details
Fission Amoeba (Binary), Plasmodium (Multiple) Cell division.
Fragmentation Spirogyra Breakage into pieces.
Regeneration Planaria, Hydra Specialized cells regrow missing parts.
Budding Hydra, Yeast Outgrowth detaches.
Veg. Prop. Potato (Eye), Bryophyllum (Leaf) New plants from roots/stem/leaf.
Tissue Culture Ornamental Plants Cells (Callus) grown in lab nutrient medium. Disease free.

2. Sexual Reproduction (Plants)

  • Unisexual Flower: Papaya, Watermelon. (Contains Stamen OR Pistil).
  • Bisexual Flower: Hibiscus, Mustard. (Contains BOTH).

Seed Formation

After Fertilization (Zygote $\to$ Embryo), the Ovule becomes a Seed.

  • Cotyledon: Food store.
  • Plumule: Future Shoot.
  • Radicle: Future Root.

3. Human Reproduction

Puberty Changes

  • Common: Hair growth (armpits/genital), Oily skin, Pimples.
  • Males: Voice cracks/deepens, Beard growth, Penis enlargement.
  • Females: Breast development, Menstruation starts.

Reproductive Systems

  • Male: Testes (Sperm/Testosterone, Scrotum keeps temp $2-3^\circ$C lower). Vas Deferens. Glands (Prostate/Seminal) for nutrition/fluidity.
  • Female: Ovary (Egg/Estrogen). Fallopian Tube (Fertilization site). Uterus (Implanation).
PLACENTA Disc-like tissue between Uterine wall and Embryo.
Structure: Villi on embryo side, Blood spaces on mother's side.
Function: Exchange of Glucose/Oxygen (Mom $\to$ Baby) and Waste (Baby $\to$ Mom).

Menstruation

If Egg not fertilized $\to$ Lining breaks $\to$ Blood/Mucus flow (2-8 days).
If Fertilized $\to$ No Menstruation (Pregnancy).

4. Reproductive Health

  • Contraception: Mechanical (Condom/Copper-T), Chemical (Pills), Surgical (Vasectomy/Tubectomy).
  • STDs: Bacterial (Gonorrhoea/Syphilis). Viral (Warts/HIV-AIDS).
SOCIAL ISSUE: SEX RATIO Misuse of ultrasound for sex-determination (Female Foeticide) has led to declining Child Sex Ratio. This upsets societal balance. Pre-natal sex determination is illegal in India.
