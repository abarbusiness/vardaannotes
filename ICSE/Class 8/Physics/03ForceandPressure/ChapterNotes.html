    $Moment = Force \times \text{Perpendicular Distance}$

    Let's avoid the verb adjectives safely. Q1. Why are broad wooden sleepers placed beneath railway tracks?

    Answer: This is to increase the area of contact. A larger area drastically decreases the massive pressure exerted by the heavy train passing over the tracks, preventing the tracks from dangerously sinking into the ground.

    Q2. Calculate the pressure if a force of $50 N$ acts on an area of $2 m^2$.

    Answer: Force = $50 N$, Area = $2 m^2$. Pressure = Force / Area = $50 / 2 = 25 Pa$.
